## Adding Team Members
To add a team member to an EventSpace, on your EventSpace dashboard, simply click on the '+' button and select the team member to add.
<Frame>
    <img height="200" src="/guides/images/+.png" />
</Frame> 

If one of your team members is not yet signed up with Revent, click on the three dots and invite them with their email.
<Frame>
    <img height="200" src="/guides/images/three_dots.png" />
</Frame>

From here, you can manage your team.
<Frame>
    <img height="200" src="/guides/images/invite.png" />
</Frame>

## Communicating with Your Team

### Overview Page
On your event's overview page, you can share notes and ideas with your team. This stays internal within your team.

<Frame>
    <img height="50" src="/guides/images/overview.png" />
</Frame>

### Additional Pages

Under the Pages navigation bar, you can create additional pages where you can collaborate with your team.
<Frame>
    <img height="200" src="/guides/images/page.png" />
</Frame>


## Collaboration Tools

Additionally, you can collaborate with teammates through tools such as Budgets and Task Boards. Please refer to the guides for [Budgets](/guides/budget) and [Task Boards](/guides/task).
