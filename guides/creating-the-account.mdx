##
To begin using Revent, you'll need to create an account. Follow these steps:
- **Sign Up**: Visit the Revent website and click on the ‘Sign Up’ button. You can register using your email, through Google OAuth, or through your GitHub account.
- **Verify Email**: Check your email for a verification link from Revent. Click the link to verify your account.
- **Initial Setup**: After verification, you’ll be directed to the Eventspaces dashboard, where you can set up your first Eventspace and start organizing events.
