title: Creating Budgets
---
In your EventSpace, the Tools section provides access to Budgets to help you efficiently manage event logistics and team collaboration. 
##### To create a new tool:
1. **Navigate to the Tools Bar**: Within your EventSpace, click on the Tools tab to view existing Budgets or to create new ones.
2. **Create New Tools**: To create a new Budget, click on the + New button in the top-right corner.

### Budgets
When creating a new Budget, you can choose from three options:
- **Featured Budgets**: Pre-made templates designed for common budgeting needs.
- **Community Budgets**: Templates shared by the community.
- **Blank Budget**: Start from scratch and customize as needed.
