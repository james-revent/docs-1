---
title: "Creating and Managing Tickets"
---

Once the event details have been set up, you can create and manage tickets for your event. 

1. **Navigate to Tickets**: Go to your Event, then select Registration from the menu, and click on Tickets.
2. **Create Ticket Types**: Here, you can create different types of tickets, each with its own pricing, availability, and descriptions.

<Frame>
    <img height="200" src="/guides/images/ticket.png" />
</Frame>

3. **Add Custom Questions**: Enhance your registration process by adding custom questions to the tickets. This feature allows you to gather valuable information from attendees.

<Frame>
    <img height="200" src="/guides/images/question.png" />
</Frame>

By customizing ticket types and adding attendee questions, you can better manage your audience and create a more tailored event experience.
