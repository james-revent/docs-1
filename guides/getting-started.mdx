### Creating an Account
Sign up using your email, Google OAuth, or GitHub. Verify your email to access the EventSpaces dashboard. Start by setting up your first EventSpace and enable two-factor authentication for added security.

### Creating an EventSpace

### Organizing Event Details
Set up event information like name, date, and description. Use the Overview page to share notes, brainstorm ideas, and keep your team aligned.

### Creating and Managing Tickets
Create and customize various ticket types with different prices, availability, and descriptions. Add custom questions to tailor the registration process.

### Creating Budgets and Task Boards
Use the budgeting tool to plan expenses and revenue for your events. Utilize Task Boards to organize tasks, assign responsibilities, and ensure smooth team collaboration and event execution.
